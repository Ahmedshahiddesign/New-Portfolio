Flight Search Engine Interface

About the client:

FCF is an online service helping find flight tickets. Unlike other relevant services, FCF's focus is not budget but comfort: it allows finding and picking the best option within minutes. However, it was lost in the background of other flight search engines that didn't have a focus like FCF.

Solution:

To help the service achieve its goal, Lazarev.team focused on adding more details to the UI. For instance, each flight option features a photo and detailed measurements of each seat, while it’s also possible to communicate preferences in more detail in the comfort/price balance bar.
